# AI Lab Assignment: Regression Models

**Objective**: You will apply regression techniques to predict a target variable using various classical AI models. The dataset remains the same (Top 1000 IMDb Movies), but now your task is to train models to predict a selected column based on relevant features.

---

## Scenario

You are a data scientist at DaciaVision Analytics. After completing the initial data preprocessing and feature engineering phase, your team is now tasked with building predictive models to forecast a key numerical variable of interest. Your goal is to analyze different regression models and compare their performance.

Your dataset: [IMDb Top 1000 Movies](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/krishna-koly/IMDB_TOP_1000/refs/heads/main/imdb_top_1000.csv)

## **Part 3: Training Regression Models (2.5 or 5 points)**

1. **Linear Regression**
2. **Decision Tree Regression**
3. **Random Forest Regression**

For each model:
- Train it using the selected features.
- Optimize hyperparameters where applicable.
- Evaluate its performance using relevant metrics (e.g., RMSE, MAE, R-squared).
- Compare model performances using a table and visualization (e.g., bar chart of RMSE values).

**max 2.5 points** – Awarded to AI enthusiasts who successfully train models and report their outputs, focusing on final results rather than deep understanding.

**max 5 points** – Awarded to experts who not only implement models but also explain their inner workings, how parameters impact predictions, and how different features contribute to the outcome.

During evaluation, you must specify which category you are in (AI-enthusiast or expert) so that grading can be applied accordingly.
